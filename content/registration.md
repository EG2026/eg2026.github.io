### Categories

**Full Conference**

Full Conference registration includes access to all main conference sessions and all tutorials.

**Student**

Student registration is a Full Conference registration at a discounted rate for students. If we cannot confirm your student status from public sources, we might ask you for proof via email.

**Retired Researchers**

TBA

**Big Buddy**

Big Buddy Ticket Includes 1 main participant and up to 2 additional students. For the additional students, name and email address are required during registration. If we cannot confirm your student status from public sources, we might ask you for proof via email.
